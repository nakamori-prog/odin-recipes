Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Peanut butter cookies are cookies made with peanut butter as a primary ingredient. This simple recipe can sate a sweet tooth and only requires a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Peanut butter
  • Sugars (both white and brown)
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Leaveners (baking soda and baking powder)
  • Salt
