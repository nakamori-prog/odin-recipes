Chicken Katsu

Description

Chicken katsu is a Japanese dish made by deep-frying chicken cutlets in an oil or other solution. It is nutritious, easy to make, and often paired with rice, cabbage, or other staples.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast halves (x4)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Oil (preferably neutral)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
