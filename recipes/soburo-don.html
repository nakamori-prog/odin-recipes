Soburo Don (Ground-Beef Bowl)

Description

Soburo don is an easy to make bento-style dish combining ground beef, egg, veggies, and rice. It is the dish of choice for children, broke students, and time-crunched salarymen.

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings (sugar, salt, mirin, sake, soy sauce)
  • Steamed rice
  • Blanched green vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook ground chicken and all the seasonings in a saucepan or frying pan.
  2. Cook scrambled eggs in a saucepan or frying pan.
  3. Serve the steamed rice in the bowl, then put seasoned ground chicken, eggs, and green vegetables over the rice.
